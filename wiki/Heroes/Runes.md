# Runes

**Runes** are hero equipment slotted in a hero's dedicated **Rune slot**. Each rune is bound to one specific **Tactical** or **Pursuit** skill and raises **that skill's trigger chance** ("Skill Trigger Probability"). There are **105** rune kinds — one per such skill.

## How runes work
- **One rune per hero.** Each hero has a single Rune slot, and a rune only helps the skill it's tied to. **There are no rune set bonuses** (unlike gear's 3-/6-piece sets — runes have no set effect).
- **Levels:** a rune's bonus scales with its level — **★4** runes go **Lv 1–4**, **★5** runes go **Lv 1–5**.
- **Must be max level to equip:** the equip list only offers fully-levelled runes, so raise a rune to its cap (Lv 4 / Lv 5) before slotting it.
- **Upgrade** a rune with **Runes-Fragment**; **dismantle** unwanted runes back into Runes-Fragment.
- **Obtain (boxes):** 4★ Runes (Random) from **Abyss** / **Union War**; 5★ Runes (Random) from **Union Store** / Abyss / Union War; 5★ Runes (Customize, you choose the skill) from **Tournament** / **Lucky Wheel** / **Lucky Mystery Box**.
- *(Server-side, not in the files: exact Runes-Fragment cost per level, dismantle yield, and box rates.)*
