## Background
You understand that being a High Mage is not Faye's end, it is merely the beginning. What Faye seeks is the ultimate magic, a magic capable of repeatedly restarting the world. Fortunately, Faye is very wise. She concealed her identity as a High Mage and mingled with various adventure teams. Almost every adventure team has, more or less, seen Faye's presence. Faye never seeks quick success. As the team's mage, she always fulfills a support role, responsible for casting various enhancement magics on her teammates. Thus, she followed one team after another, experiencing adventure after adventure. Her footsteps never ceased, gradually traversing mountains and rivers.

Her willpower and obsession transformed into the guiding light on her path. After perceiving the essence of magic, Faye felt she was getting closer to the essence of the world. She gradually sensed the world's barrier, and gradually became aware of the figure that had always been watching her. Was that a god? She wasn't sure. You have also glimpsed her desolation from afar when she hit a wall. Faye attempted countless times to influence something, but the barrier was never shattered. It was like an insurmountable wall. Finally, after countless setbacks, Faye's dedication to researching magic gradually became fervent. She read every ancient grimoire, even digging out those that had been gathering dust for who knows how many years. The path of seeking the truth eventually echoed. Later, as the City Lord, you crowned her with the highest title every mage dreams of — the God of Magic.

No one else in the world could match her magical attainments. God of Magic, meaning that in the realm of magic, she was akin to a deity. The rules of the domain finally revealed their true form before her eyes. She had touched the pinnacle of magic and could finally alter some things, however slightly. The legend truly belonging to her officially began at this moment. That day, she cast the most powerful magic, affecting the entire world. The world's mana concentration rose by who knows how many levels. Naturally, all the glory and praise surged towards her. People believed it was the blessing of the God of Magic, and they chanted the name of the God of Magic.
