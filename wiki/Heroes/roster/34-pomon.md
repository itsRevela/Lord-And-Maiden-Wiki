## Background
If asked who is the most dominant in the Holy Knight Order, it would undoubtedly be Roland. But if asked who is the most fearsome, the third knight "Calamity Star" Pomon deserves that title. Pomon adores black, the color of night. Her power accumulates in battle, her spear aimed only at enemies. Scorching sweat droplets splatter onto her black gown, spreading like ink before long.

In her childhood, Pomon had once been a wanderer. Her "waa waa" cries stirred compassion in others, until a kind-hearted, childless noble from the area took her home. The lady of the house treated her exceedingly well, giving her a warm childhood to remember.

But the barbarians' invasion shattered her peaceful life. She barely escaped with her life, thus beginning her long wandering journey. Since then, misfortune always followed wherever she went, earning her the epithet "Calamity Star."

It wasn't until she joined the Holy Knight Order that her weary heart was reignited by fighting spirit. She realized that if misfortune originated from the world's chaos, then the only way to restore order was through battle.
