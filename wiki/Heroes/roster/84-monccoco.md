## Background
The rejuvenating spring always makes people reluctant to wake up. When Monccoco turned off her alarm clock once again, the teacher had already marked her name with a big cross on the attendance sheet. "Monccoco, this is already the fourth time you've been late this week, and we've only had four days of classes." "I'm... I'm sorry, Teacher Halena, I didn't mean to." After speaking, Monccoco rubbed her eyes and couldn't help but yawn. She didn't know what was wrong with her—she felt so tired and sleepy, even though she had already slept for a long, long time.

Tonight, Monccoco is determined not to sleep too deeply, aiming to wake up early at least once before the week ends. Perhaps the psychological suggestion worked, as this time she clearly saw her own dream. The world in the dream was bizarre and surreal—one moment, lovers were meeting under a beautiful moon, and the next, she was being chased by a ferocious beast, teetering on the edge of danger. It felt as if she had entered someone else's dream.

Unsurprisingly, she was late again the next day. When she told Teacher Halena everything that had happened the previous night, Halena was overjoyed. If the girl wasn't lying, this might be the initial reaction of one of the advanced light magics—"Sleep."

Finally, under Halena's intense training, Monccoco gradually mastered the methods of falling asleep and waking up. Sometimes she even felt that staying awake was much easier than sleeping.
