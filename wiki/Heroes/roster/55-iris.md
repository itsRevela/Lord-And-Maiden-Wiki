In her world, every leaf has its own story, and every flower has its own secret. She is full of love and reverence for nature, and has established deep friendships with the creatures in the forest.

In her opinion, as a superior being, the Red Elf should pay more attention to self - cultivation and moral conduct, rather than indulging in their instincts like wild beasts.

However, Lilith's behavior was like a thorn, piercing deeply into her heart. Lilith indulged her instincts, wallowed in desires, and even took pleasure in hunting and killing the creatures in the forest. This made Iris feel a deep sense of shame.
