Suddenly, a wounded white dove fell at her feet, with a blood - stained letter tied to its leg. She unfolded the letter, which read: "In the Shadow Forest, the children need you."

She immediately mounted her horse and rushed towards the Shadow Forest, feeling a sense of unease. Faint cries came from the depths of the forest. She followed the sound and found several people in black robes surrounding a group of trembling children. The black - robed men sneered and said, "You managed to escape fifteen years ago, but not this time."

The battle was on the verge of breaking out. Aguria charged into the enemy ranks without hesitation. The weapons in her hands turned into two silver lights, and each stab took the life of a demon. She was so fast that the enemies couldn't even catch sight of her.

Finally, the men in black robes dispersed in the light. Aguria picked up a crying little girl and comforted her softly, "Don't be afraid. I'm here." Looking at the smiling faces of the children who regained their freedom, she understood the meaning of her perseverance - to dispel darkness with light and protect everyone in need.
