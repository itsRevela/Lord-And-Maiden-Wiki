## Background
Why does the Ulito Restaurant keep operating forever without ever closing? This has a great deal to do with the City Lord. As the place with the highest foot traffic in the entire city, there is hardly any information that cannot be found here. And this Ulito Restaurant, abruptly appearing in the city center, is even more intriguing. It's not that Yaya appears at the Ulito Restaurant every night. More often than not, as the Chief Intelligence Officer, Yaya needs to travel to other cities and kingdoms to gather intelligence. At such times, Yaya removes her bunny girl attire and changes back into a gorgeous banquet dress. This is another identity of Yaya's, of course, also a fabricated one, aimed solely at better infiltrating banquets. Banquets are always held at night, and she is always the most gorgeous focal point of the entire event.

After each mission ends, Yaya discards the heavy accessories on her. These seemingly gorgeous ornaments are actually worthless, all cheap imitations that can be bought for just a few copper coins. Thanks to Yaya's outfit, no one questions the authenticity of the accessories. For most of her mission time, Yaya wears a brilliant smile. But only when the mission is settled, when Yaya receives a big bag of gold coins as payment and hears the clinking sound of the coins, does she reveal a genuine smile. Although Yaya is the owner of the Ulito Restaurant, most of its operating income must be handed over to the City Lord. In reality, Yaya is very poor. Having been poor for so long, she has become increasingly afraid of poverty. Only at night, lying amidst a pile of gold coins, can she find a moment of peace.

The greater reason for Yaya's poverty is that almost after every mission, she always donates the few gold coins she has on her. She believes many people need this money more than she does. The people here really like Miss Yaya. In places where the sunlight shines, everyone likes Miss Yaya, and Miss Yaya always smiles at them. Whether at the restaurant, in shops, or at banquets, Yaya is always the most dazzling one. This is our Yaya, the cutest and most well-behaved one. Wishing Miss Yaya happiness every day. — Recorded on a certain page of the City Lord's diary.
