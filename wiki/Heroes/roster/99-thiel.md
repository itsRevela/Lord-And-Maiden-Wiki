However, a war conflict among humans shattered the peace. She could only watch helplessly as her adoptive parents were tortured to death in the war, and their soul energy scattered into the air. Thiel, hiding in the corner, saw the hideous - faced soldiers, as well as the war - torn land and dilapidated houses. At that moment, she saw through the hypocrisy and cruelty beneath the power of light of the human race. The orcish aura hidden in her body was triggered, and her black pupils turned bright red.

After transforming, Thiel couldn't control her power well. She wantonly destroyed everything around her, venting the pain caused by the suddenly surging power within her body. It was not until she met Slider that Slider gave Thiel a sharp sword emitting a red glow, and the restless power in her body gradually subsided. This sharp sword, which needs to be nourished by the soul power of living beings, is a double - edged sword for Thiel. While Thiel's power is increasing, her desire for soul power is also growing, and she may even lose her reason occasionally. However, how could she give up the power that can destroy the light?

She has no faith. Her heart consumed by evil is just an excellent weapon in the eyes of Slider. During the day, she hides in various corners, and the dark night is the stage where she starts her hunting.
