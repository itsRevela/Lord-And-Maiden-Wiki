## Background
Sera's family perished in a demon tide. With his dying breath, her father entrusted her with the cursed sword "Iron Devourer": "Never let it starve." She soon discovered the blade consumed metal to grow stronger—but with each use, crimson patterns would creep from the sword onto her skin.

Wandering as a mercenary, Sera survived by slaying demons with the blade. Until the day Lafayette, the Sixth Knight of the Holy Knights, revealed the truth: the sword was feeding on fragments of the Spirit-Sealing Steles—it hungered not for metal, but for the curses imprisoning demons.

Now Sera serves as a blade against the Dibuk cult. Though the sword's patterns have claimed half her right arm, she sneers: "Better to coexist with this ravenous demon than die."
