## Background
A century ago, when the family was at the height of its prominence, Perris and Olrun crossed paths at a ball hosted by a noble. Back then, she said, "On the day the new moon rises, we will meet again."

On the day the new moon arrived, Olrun came as promised. Looking at the girl sitting blankly by the window, she said with a pained expression, "Perris, why do you look so haggard now?" After recognizing who had come, Perris lowered her head, covering her right face, and gritted her teeth, saying, "It's over! Everything is over! Those bastards! The seal has loosened. My face is injured too! What should I do?"

Olrun looked at the woman with a troubled expression and said, "I do know of a red elf named Lilith in the southwest who might be able to help you, but... you may need to pay a price."

Perris's expression gradually calmed, as if she were still the proud and elegant lady from the ball. "As long as I can gain power, I am willing to pay any price." When she reappeared in people's sight, Perris was even more beautiful than before. This time, she was here to recruit maids.
