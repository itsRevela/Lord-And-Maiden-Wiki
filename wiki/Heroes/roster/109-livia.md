## Background
Livia lived in a mysterious forest isolated from the world since she was a child. Under the guidance of her grandmother, she learned to use a magical magic book. Before her death, her grandmother warned Livia to use power carefully to avoid disasters . Although Livia respects her grandmother's teachings, she is eager to show her abilities.

She began to travel, looking for people in need. One time, she came to a small town, got acquainted with the lord there, and assisted him in handling various affairs. Under the temptation of power, she gradually lost herself. Until she discovered that the lord was trying to use her power to control the residents of the city for endless energy.

At the critical moment, she remembered her grandmother's teachings and realized that power should be used to protect the innocent. She uncovered the plot of the lord and saved the inhabitants. Since then, she is determined to use the power of the magic book only when necessary to defend justice and innocent lives. She focuses on the underprivileged. She is eager to use her strength to defend justice and bring peace to the world.

She constantly improves her abilities and speaks out for the weak. She vowed to always remember her grandmother's teachings and use the power of the magic book for the just cause. Although the road ahead is full of challenges, she firmly believes that as long as the faith is firm, the dark forces will eventually be dispelled.
