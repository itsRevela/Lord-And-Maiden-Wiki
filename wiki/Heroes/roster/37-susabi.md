## Background
The nine-tailed fox is an extremely rare species among the beastmen, and Susabi's arrival undoubtedly caused quite a stir.

That day, the beautiful nine-tailed girl spoke with the clan leader for a long time. No one knew what they discussed, but it was clear that the leader emerged from the room with a grave expression, while the girl followed closely behind, her face as unreadable as when she had arrived.

After that, no one ever saw Susabi among the beastmen again. According to some older beastmen, they had briefly encountered Susabi in their childhood. Back then, Susabi's appearance was not much different from now, and they were just as elusive.

Some say she only appears during times of crisis for the beastmen, while others claim she was entrusted by the first clan leader to travel between continents, seeking the secrets of the world.
