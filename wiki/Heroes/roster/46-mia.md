## Background
Unlike Lafayette's healing abilities, Mia's wings had almost no effect on wounds. A few days before Lafayette left, she visited Mia.

"Mia, I'm going out to explore the world. Will you come with me? You're so powerful, you could definitely save more people." "I can't leave yet. I have a feeling Vanessa will harm our people. If that's the case, I need to stay and protect everyone." Mia's expression was serious, but her gaze was firm.

"Alright, Mia, I respect your choice, but if there's any danger, please be sure to send me a letter." Mia cherished the days spent living with her tribe. She didn't believe that the conflicts of the outside world wouldn't affect the beastmen's lives. The world is interconnected, and it's just that the flames of war haven't reached here yet.
