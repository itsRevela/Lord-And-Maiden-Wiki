## Background
As the soul of the Holy Knights, Roland always kept an eye on the changes in the world. It wasn't until much later that Roland reflected on why he had joined the Holy Knights—it must have been because he was brainwashed by Utija's motivational speeches. But in the end, the facts proved that Utija was right.

After witnessing poverty and hardship, and experiencing betrayal and covert struggles, she had indeed grown. Although she often clashed with Utija due to their differing approaches, neither of them ever forced the other to agree with their views. Many might not know, but in private, they were truly each other's confidants.

In her spare time, she would always sit at the highest point of the city walls, gazing at the town. During these moments, countless thoughts would flash through her mind—sometimes analyzing the situation, other times pondering human nature. At home, Roland was a somewhat picky but easygoing young lady, yet when facing monsters, she was calm and skilled in battle.
