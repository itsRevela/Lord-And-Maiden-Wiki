## Background
Orlando is the spiritual eye of the Paladins. As a psychic, she can not only communicate with all psychic things, but also see the future through her aqua blue eyes. But what the spiritual eye can reach is not specific, just some symbolic symbols and pictures. When the premonition is strong, her spear will also sense and indicate the direction.

But this is far from enough, and it takes Utija's guidance to find the answer. When she was passing through a border town, she had sensed that a huge disaster was about to happen in the town. But instead this hunch disappeared, and instead, the town was about to usher in vigorous development.

The two extremely divided futures have tortured the immature prophet Orlando, and her mental strength has been severely damaged. Before we can make the right choice, disaster has already come to the town in advance. The raging fire spread in the town, and Orlando, trapped by the fire, was severely overdrawn. Hearing people's pain, she was so anxious but could do nothing.

This disaster made Orlando doubt his own existence and ability, and he was once outside the world, afraid to face reality. But Yutika told her that the prophecy was the will of God, and the unexpected arrival of disaster was just a punishment for those who deviate from God.
