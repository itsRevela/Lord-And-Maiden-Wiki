Satoru had sat on the edge of the high platform countless times. She could hear the whispers of the wind and feel the raindrops, but she couldn't see the shape of the world. She didn't know when it started, but the feeling of anticipation had disappeared. Happiness or sadness meant nothing to her.

She had thought that her heart would never stir again. However, when the seal of the Spirit - Sealing Monument was damaged and Kurorand fell into chaos, the soft whispers of Lafayette finally awakened the memories that had been sealed deep in her heart.

She said, "Satoru, wait a little longer. I've found a way to cure your eyes. Once the demons are sealed, we'll definitely be able to return to the good old days." At that moment, Satoru heard the sound of her own heartbeat. So, she decided to look forward to it again.
