## Background
Rhea still remembers the scene when her father left. Five hundred years ago, Rhea was still very young. Her father lifted her onto his shoulder with one hand and lovingly patted her head, telling her that when he returned, he would take little Rhea to the most open place to count the stars.

Rhea looked around, but in the end, all she received was the shield and armor left by her father. Five hundred years passed, and the monsters began to stir once more. Without hesitation, Rhea chose to join the army under the Holy Knights. Over the years, she had never stopped training herself, all for the day she could inherit her father's will.

Rhea modified her father's armor and wore it herself. Before setting off, she took the Mountain Shield that had accompanied her father on the battlefield. This time, she vowed to eradicate the monsters once and for all.
