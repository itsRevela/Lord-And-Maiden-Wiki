## Background
Among all the children, the clan leader favored his eldest daughter, Vanessa, the most. She possessed extraordinary talent. While other children struggled to learn a spell in a week, she could master it in less than a day. Her privileged background and exceptional intelligence gave her reason to be proud.

She gradually distanced herself from the tribe, weary of beastmen her age who couldn't match her excellence and resentful of her father's repetitive teachings. Until one day, her father's harsh slap completely awakened the beast sleeping within Vanessa. She knew they might never understand.

So she stopped caring about others' feelings, stopped wasting her time trying to meet their expectations, and decided to be herself. Perhaps this would lead her to a different life. She set her sights on the Dibuk organization.

Legend has it that on the day Vanessa arrived, a light drizzle fell from the sky. Dressed in a stunning black cheongsam, she looked peerlessly elegant. After the rain cleared, the town took on a new radiance, as if the past had been washed away by the rain, and a new Dibuk organization was born.
