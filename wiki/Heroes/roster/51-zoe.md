## Background
Cold-blooded and powerful, no one is more suited to be a night watcher than Zoe. She enjoys lurking in the darkness, as it is the best disguise. Half a month ago, the head of the Susa swordsmithing family forged the peerless demon sword Higan. Overnight, all factions coveted it. Although Olrun herself had little interest, there were always employers willing to offer high rewards, and the task naturally fell to Zoe.

When Zoe arrived at the Susa family estate, it was already in chaos. It took her three full days to track down SusaMaki's trail. The girl was more formidable than she had imagined, and the power of the demon sword gradually merged with her. The fierce battle lasted all night, but at dawn, the girl still managed to escape.

"So, the demon sword has accepted SusaMaki? What a pity. Now I have to think carefully about whether to continue the mission," Olrun said, nibbling on her left index finger, visibly conflicted. "I can find her." "Mhm, of course. But Zoe, after some time, her compatibility with the demon sword will be even higher. Even if you defeat her, the sword won't be what the employer wants anymore. What do you think?"

Zoe's grip on the hilt slowly loosened. She understood what Olrun meant—the employer was responsible for providing the reward, and she was responsible for getting the job done. The rest was none of her concern. Since there was no need to continue the mission, she might as well take a break. That girl's strikes were no lighter than her own.
