## Background
The spiritual power fluctuations of the Spirit Sealing Monument became increasingly intense, and it seemed as if the entire earth was trembling because of it. Utija, the Time Priestess, has shouldered a heavy responsibility ever since she inherited that mysterious magic power five hundred years ago.

Her heart is often troubled by the abnormal changes in the celestial phenomena. Those strange lights and twinkling stars seem to be foreshadowing the significant changes she is about to face.

In recent days, Utija has found it even more difficult to fall asleep. She is haunted by those nightmares. The villages devoured by the raging fire and the ruins filled with billowing smoke seem to keep reappearing in her mind. Those worlds are completely devoid of life, filled with the stench of death and despair, making her feel extremely depressed and terrified.

Even so, Utija has never given up hope. She is well aware that the prophecy in the divine revelation is not groundless — "When the seal is broken, monsters will rule the world. The cycle of destiny will turn, and those who go against the common will appear, and the situation will change." These words are like a sharp sword, constantly reminding her of the heavy responsibility on her shoulders. She knows that she must be prepared for the arrival of the destined one.
