## Background
Recently, a new dancer arrived in Umiga, and in no time, numerous nobles and royals traveled great distances, spending fortunes just to have the girl perform a solo dance for them. Her vibrant long skirt twirled with her steps, her fair and slender hands shifting into various poses in the air. Even strands of her hair seemed infused with soul, every movement captivating the heart.

Half a year ago, Thefis woke up in a small cabin in the forest, having forgotten many things. She could only vaguely recall a place called "City of Fallen Dust." She danced instinctively and discovered that her dance could weave illusions based on the desires of those watching. Seeing them cry or laugh, Thefis felt only envy in her hollow heart.

Later, someone told her that Crocodile, Vanessa's trusted general from back then, seemed to have a connection to the "City of Fallen Dust." Perhaps Crocodile could provide her with clues to her memories.
