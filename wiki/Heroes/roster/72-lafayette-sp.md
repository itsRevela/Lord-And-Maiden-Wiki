## Background
Lafayette stood in the healing hall of the Holy Knights, a soft glow emanating from the feather in her hand. With a gentle wave, the knight's wounds quickly healed, leaving no scars. This had become her daily routine since becoming the sixth knight, using the power of feathers to heal many. Yet, there was always a void in her heart—the forest, and the ever-sleepy Bast.

At night, Lafayette sat alone in the garden, holding the feather and lost in thought. Elder Cliodna's words before she left echoed in her heart: "Healing is not just about mending wounds, but also about healing hearts. If you feel lost, come back and see." Lafayette froze, a strong longing welling up inside her.

After everything was over, Lafayette returned to the forest. She approached the small cabin and saw Bast still lazily sprawled at the door, her eyes half-open. "You're back," Bast said in a drowsy voice, as if Lafayette had never left. Lafayette smiled, lit a stick of incense, and sat down beside her.

Moonlight spilled over the small cabin as Lafayette softly began to tell a story. She knew she had finally found true healing—not just for others, but for herself as well.
