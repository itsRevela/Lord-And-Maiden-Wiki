## Background
There is no elf like Dolly who can communicate silently with nature. Whether it is trees, flowers and plants, or fish and birds, you can be infected by Dolly's enthusiasm and be impressed by her.

Dolly especially likes to fly and inhabit the forest, and feel the fresh and free breath, which makes her form a unique natural power, which has the ability to heal and revive at the same time.

Dolly! Stop!" The elf elder was a little out of breath as he watched the weeds he had carefully cleaned up were revived by Dolly's arrow. But Dolly obviously didn't care about the elder's emotions, and instantly made the bare land green.

Because Dolly is free, she also believes that all things are also free, and their growth should not be stifled, and she also believes that "usefulness" should not be the standard of nature. Her unique ideas have not been accepted by others, but she is still implementing her views with actions, and everywhere she goes is the breath of life.
