## Background
"Aguria! Have you seen my hair tie? It was just on the table!" Aguria, half-awake and squinting, said with a nasal tone from just waking up, "Isn't it on your wrist?" Synaga paused for two seconds, then said, "Oh, right."

Similar incidents happen almost every day. Synaga's terrible memory often drives the other members of the Holy Knights crazy, so before every mission, Roland always reminds her a few extra times to make sure she hasn't forgotten any details.

However, once she enters work mode, Synaga seems like a completely different person. Her sharp, unapproachable aura and precise, decisive moves undoubtedly prove to the world her exceptional strength as the fourth knight of the Holy Knights.

Recently, Synaga received a new mission issued by Saintess Shin - to protect the newly arrived city lord. Although she doesn't know what's so special about that person, she will definitely risk her life to complete every mission she receives.
