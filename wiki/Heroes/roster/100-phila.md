## Background
Phila has always been the most outstanding one in the Winteria Family. Since the fall of their ancestors, no member of the Winteria Family has been able to join the Paladin Order for a hundred years, and the family has even been in decline. As a hybrid of human and elf, Phila's awakened power has brought honor and vitality to the declining Winteria Family.

On the day Phila was born, layers of holy light enveloped her. The family elders, hearing the news, rushed over and took her away. At that moment, the equipment sealed in the secret chamber also emitted strands of blue light, resonating with the power within Phila. This brought great surprise to the Winteria family.

To this day, Phila does not know what her parents looked like. The elders of the family remain tight-lipped about it, only urging her day after day to study and train without pause. As a child, Phila was often punished for moments of laziness or injured by uncontrollable equipment. But the elders warned her not to fail and trained her even more harshly. During rare moments of rest, she wanted to get closer to other tribe members, but they always kept their distance. Over time, Phila grew to reject closeness with others, even becoming tsundere and keeping people at arm's length.

Becoming a member of the Paladin Order is not very important to Phila. It's just to fulfill the mission of her family. The task of being an envoy to the Paladin Order has made her witness the insignificance and fragility of humans, as well as their genuine emotions. Although she shows disdain and is merciless in her sarcasm, her lonely heart also has a soft side, longing for that bit of intimacy.
