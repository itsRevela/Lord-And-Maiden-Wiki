## Background
When she grew up, Duffy chose to join Mia's legion. She hoped that the living environment in the world could be better, so that these children could always be happy.

Duffy stood on the training ground of the legion. Hearing the laughter and playing of children in the distance, she frowned. Since joining Mia's legion, she hadn't been home for a long time, and she also hadn't seen that little tail who was always clinging to her, Darby, for a long time.

One day, the legion received a mission to escort refugees. Duffy was busy among the crowd when she suddenly heard a familiar voice: "Sister!" She turned around and saw Darby running towards her with an excited smile on her face. "I joined the medical team," Darby said proudly. "I want to be like you, sister, and help those in need."

Duffy was stunned. The little girl who used to always cling to her in her memory had now grown up and had her own dreams. She watched Darby's busy figure, and a warm current welled up in her heart.
