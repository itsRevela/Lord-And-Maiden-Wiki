## Background
Nyx was once a court apothecary, her potions and healing spells hailed as the “Grace of the Sacred Tree.” There was hardly anyone who didn’t seek her aid. Yet those nobles who survived never dared meet her gaze again—because her salvation never came without a cost.

When a great plague struck, Nyx defied royal law by secretly studying and applying the forbidden elven tome, The Nocturne, attempting to use the ancient soul-transference ritual to cure the afflicted.

She did save many lives, but was condemned for her actions. Before being exiled, she left these parting words: “If the sun abandons you—let the night protect you instead.”

Now, Nyx has established her own Silent Altar in the borderlands. She no longer serves any crown—only the will of the wounded and her own judgment. Her powers stem from sacrifice and ancient curses, chilling to behold. Yet everyone who walks out of her sanctuary lives. She is no saint, but you will want her on your side.
