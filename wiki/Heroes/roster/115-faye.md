## Background
Faye was born in the Moonlight Grove, the oldest sanctuary of the elves, and was chosen by the Star of Prophecy as a magical prodigy. From an early age, she demonstrated astonishing insight into arcane knowledge. Her mentor once said, “Her understanding of magic feels as if it were etched into her soul.”

After her coming-of-age ceremony, Faye chose to leave her homeland and embark on a journey to find the Stellarium Codex—a legendary relic said to hold the ancient truths of elven magic.She left her mark across countless ruins, unraveling enchantments, conquering magical beasts, and even dueling with otherworldly archmages.

Now, she stands as one of the youngest High Mages in the entire Alliance. Her “Arcane Missiles” are not only symbols of destruction but also crystallizations of intellect.
