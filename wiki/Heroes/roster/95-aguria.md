## Background
To become the second knight of the Paladin Order, Aguria not only has exceptional talent but also a willpower beyond ordinary people. Anyone who has come into contact with her cannot help but give her the nickname "Little Sun." She truly is like sunlight, always bringing vitality and hope to others. However, only Roland in the entire Paladin Order knows that she once went through a dark period.

After her hometown was occupied by monsters, Aguria was locked in a pitch-black cell with the other children from her village. However, she noticed that after some time, people would come to take away some of the children, and no one knew where they went.

Finally, the day came for Aguria. She was sold to an underground organization. Life there was extremely hard, but she didn't yield. A plan was brewing in her heart. No one expected that this unremarkable little girl could bring an end to this dark organization. After that, Aguria was in a state of being pursued by the enemies for a long time, until she met the High Priest at a moment when her life was hanging by a thread.

The High Priest gave her a stable home, taught her magic, and also instilled in her optimism and resilience. Now, a century after the High Priest's passing, Aguria remains true to her original intentions. She has inherited the High Priest's legacy and is determined to spread the power of light to the world.
