"Chidaru? What is he doing here?" She knew him as the most spiritually powerful and ruthless yet most favored among all the adopted children. "Olrun sent me to tell you - you're banished. Olrun has no use for trash." Each of Chidaru's words struck Puona's heart like a hammer. So Olrun would truly cast her out, without even granting her a final meeting.

"I don't believe it! Chidaru, I need to hear it from her directly... Ah-!" "Shut up, worthless trash!" Before she could finish speaking, Chidaru enveloped Puona in a beam of light, attempting to seal and banish her.

ears streamed down her cheeks. "So... was I truly an unwanted existence all along?" The light shattered instantly. Puona seemed to see her childhood self. A tremendous surge of spirit power erupted suddenly, fragmenting memories of the past that encircled both Chidaru and Puona. Against all odds... she had succeeded. The spirit power she now possessed could surpass all her peers.

Yet, how ironic - her heart had been utterly shattered. After escaping, she transformed into her childhood self and hid deep in the mountains. She feared human contact, yet yearned to be needed. She could never forget that moment when she told her, "Don't be afraid, you have a home now."
