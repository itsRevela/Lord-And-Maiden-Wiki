## Background
Lumi is a rare "Twilight Elf" - a hybrid of elf and human. Her very existence is taboo, as twilight elves are considered disruptors of nature's balance.

Abandoned under the "Tree of Life" in Merlinseath Town as an infant, she was secretly raised by Utia. Her childhood was symbiotically tied to the ancient tree. Utia never spoke of her origins, only calling her "a child blessed by moonlight."

At age twelve, Lumi discovered a tattered diary deep in the roots, its pages scrawled with: "Child of Twilight, your bloodline is the curse."

That night, thorn-like dark patterns bloomed on her palms as vines erupted from the earth, nearly devouring the entire garden. With a sigh, Utia sealed her powers, warning her never to enter the southern forest's "Slumbering Grounds."
