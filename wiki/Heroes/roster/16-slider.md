## Background
Slider, who inherited the purest bloodline of the Dark Elves, refused to submit to her brother's rule. She left her family early to pursue absolute power. She loved the adrenaline rush that came with battle.

Vanessa had sought her out. Although she didn't want to be bothered by troublesome people, to be honest, she didn't dislike Vanessa's personality—ambitious and resourceful, with some surprising similarities to herself.

But in the end, they were not the same kind of people. Slider knew that Vanessa was a complete "madwoman." If she didn't have something she wanted to protect, she might have truly joined the Dibuk organization. But in this world, there are no "what ifs."
