## Background
Derifa hoped that her younger sister could remain pure and kind forever. Her 3-year-old sister might not have any memories, but at 5 years old, Derifa could never forget the days when they were homeless on the streets, enduring scorn and going hungry for three days straight. Back then, she vowed that no matter what, she would ensure a happy life for her sister.

Fortunately, she met the kind orphanage director, Bartlett. At that time, her sister seemed to be living happily, with peaceful and content days, but she always felt it was far from enough. She was always yearning for more.

On the day of the incident, her sister, as she had done on countless days before, dragged her to the mountains to pick wild fruits. In the evening, she lazily lingered, not wanting to leave. But what they didn't expect was that when they returned, the entire village was gone. When peace was shattered, everything happened so suddenly.

She had no choice but to continue wandering with her sister, eventually seeking refuge at the magic academy. She got her wish and moved into a large house with her sister, learning wind magic from Sylph at the academy. Although her sister was as mischievous and playful as ever, she couldn't shake the feeling that her sister had changed. Perhaps when she became powerful enough to overcome any obstacle, she would see her sister's carefree smile again.
