## Background
Bettina is a very special existence in the orc race. Bettina's golden eyes are as gentle and smooth as water, and her stature is also petite and lovely, and her deceptive appearance will always make the enemy take it lightly. There are many competitions among the younger generation in the clan, arrogance, belligerence, and unwillingness to admit defeat are their innate natures.

But Bettina is different, she yearns for peace and tranquility, and is even more reluctant to participate in fights and wars. This made many clansmen have prejudice and dissatisfaction with her, and there were not a few who came to provoke her. Thiel is one of them. One of the famous battles in the clan is the battle between Thiel and Bettina in the forest. No one had really fought with Bettina before, she was always unilaterally defending and evading.

This battle allowed many clansmen to see the true power of Bettina, and they couldn't help but feel scared. At first, Bettina passed by Thiel, who was wrapped in evil thoughts, and stepped forward with kindness to relieve Thiel's pain. But when Thiel saw Bettina, who had an extremely bright heart, he thought of his foolish belief in the light before. At this moment, Thiel was full of evil spirits and evil thoughts.

Facing such a pressing Thiel, Bettina's defense was completely unstoppable. Seeing that she was about to be pierced by a sharp blade in her throat, Bettina's self-defense weapon, the Two-Tailed Halberd, exploded with great power. After the war, almost no one of the same generation in the clan looked for Bettina to provoke trouble. Because even as strong as Thiel can't win.
