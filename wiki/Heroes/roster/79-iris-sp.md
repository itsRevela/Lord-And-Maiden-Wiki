## Background
Iris decided not to remain silent any longer. She began to influence her clan in her own way, proving with her actions that the nobility of the Red Elves was not in vain. She built a quiet dwelling deep in the forest and invited those Red Elves who were willing to follow her to come and practice.

She taught them how to listen to the voice of nature, how to restrain their inner desires, and how to seek true power in tranquility. Her dwelling gradually became a pure land, attracting many Red Elves who were confused about the current situation.

They began to pay more attention to their self - cultivation and moral conduct, striving to restrain their instincts and natural tendencies. The creatures in the forest also felt this change. They no longer feared the Red Elves but instead regarded these gradually awakening superior beings with friendly eyes.

Finally, Iris' perseverance paid off. Under her leadership, the Red Elf clan took on a new look. They were no longer slaves to their desires but guardians of nature.
