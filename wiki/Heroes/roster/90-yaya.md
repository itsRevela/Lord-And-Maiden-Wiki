## Background
The nights in Uritto are colorful. Whether you're a pampered and picky noble lady or a resourceful merchant boss, you can find your favorite dish at the Uritto Restaurant.

Few people know that besides being the most popular bunny girl in the restaurant, Yaya is also the behind-the-scenes owner of this restaurant. The more crowded and gossipy a place is, the greater the amount of information there is. There is no information that Yaya can't dig up, and at the same time, no one can force Yaya to provide information she doesn't want to share.

Yaya loves the sound of gold coins clashing, but she also clearly knows that life is more important than money. She can always strike the right balance. She doesn't miss any information she should know, and she never mentions what she shouldn't. Her financial backers have a love-hate relationship with her, and she herself really enjoys this life filled with both danger and hope.
