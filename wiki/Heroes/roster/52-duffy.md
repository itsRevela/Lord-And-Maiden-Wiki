## Background
Duffy, who is deeply influenced by Darby, really can't stand children. Duffy still remembers the day when her parents suddenly brought the young Darby in front of her. Darby looked chubby, pink, and kind of cute. But for a long time after that, Duffy's world was never quiet again.

"Hey, I'm saying, can you stop following me? Are you a little tag - along?" The young Darby couldn't understand what her sister was saying at all. She staggered along behind her sister and said with effort as she walked, "Sis... ter, hug." Duffy sighed and shook her head. Finally, she helplessly poked Darby's chubby cheek and then picked up the little girl.

Darby is extremely clingy, especially to her, which troubles Duffy a great deal. Because at the request of their parents, she has to spend a lot more time every day accompanying Darby to play some boring games.

Of course, Darby can be really cute sometimes. For example, she would save a portion of her favorite candies for her sister and let her sister play with her newly - bought toys first. Even though Duffy doesn't like to play with these pink things that only little girls would love.
