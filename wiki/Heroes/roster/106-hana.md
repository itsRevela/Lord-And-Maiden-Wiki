## Background
As the most favored youngest daughter of the patriarch of the orc clan, her character is far from that of the orc clan. In Hana's world, everything has a pure and kind heart. Hana was very weak when she was born, her breathing was not stable, and she almost lost her life. Under the careful care of her tribe, Hana developed a pure and kind side.

As long as Hana wants, she will get it, but she always wants to go to the outside world to see. Until the day when she became an adult, Hana avoided the crowd looking for her and accidentally touched the forbidden area of the clan. A long-handled ax flew towards her, the forbidden ground's magnetic field reacted too strongly, and Hana fainted to the ground before she had time to think about it. This was the last memory in Hana's mind after waking up. She tried to pick up the weapon that came with her, but it was extremely easy.

Anyone who trespasses into the forbidden land will be punished, but Hana was not only spared punishment—the entire tribe, including the clan leader, ceased all warfare to pray for her. Even Bettina, the most unique member of the tribe, was curious. How could someone so innocent and delicate make the proud, arrogant, and warlike beastmen submit to her?

Some say it's because Hana's mother was the supreme hero of the beastmen tribe, while others claim it's because the blue blood within Hana is incredibly rare... But what the true truth is, no one knows.
