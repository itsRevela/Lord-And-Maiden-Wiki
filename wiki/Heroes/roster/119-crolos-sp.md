## Background
Crolos has been feeling very sleepy lately and has realized she has lost many memories. The image of a young girl in a wedding dress lingers persistently in her memory. Crolos flips through her diary and finds the handwriting in many places gradually fading. Crolos realizes that even words can erode over time. Memories are gradually fading, so what proof is there of her existence in this world? How much time will pass before her existence is completely erased by time? Crolos decides to visit her old friends, and of course, her past self. She puts on the new clothes prepared for her by Mousika. Perhaps time is running out. If so, perhaps now is the most suitable moment to activate the ultimate time magic. And so, she returned to the past.

Crolos returned to the time when she was learning magic from Utija. Watching her younger self, Crolos hid behind a tree, feeling immensely grateful to see Teacher Utija again. As the lesson ended, after sending the young Crolos home, Utija pulled Crolos out from behind the tree. "It seems your time is running out," Utija said, patting Crolos's head. Crolos suddenly hugged Utija tightly. "Teacher, I know, but I don't want to leave yet. There are still so many, so many adventures left unfinished, I still want to go," Crolos wept, tears falling uncontrollably. Utija just smiled, seemingly not saddened by this. Perhaps things weren't as bad as Crolos thought.

Crolos returned to the time when she worked as a scholar's assistant in the kingdom. She was full of energy back then, always eager to explore everywhere. She once again met that young girl in the wedding dress—Niya. She witnessed all her adventures with her again and finally understood what those lost memories were. She couldn't influence time, but she knew herself. She wrote her story with her once more into that diary. Until later, when she spoke with the town lord about that beautiful story filled with little white flowers. Crolos returned to that town once again and, on that night, saw Niya again. "See, I haven't forgotten you!" Niya said. "It seems you haven't forgotten me either," Niya said, giving Crolos a big hug.

It seems Crolos is no longer resisting the passage of time. Of course, since Crolos, who possesses time magic, can return to any point in time, It also means Crolos lives forever in all times, even if her memories differ. However, those who remember Crolos—Utija, Mousika, Niya, and others—will remember her forever. This world, she once came here. This world holds her adventures, her bonds, her memories. Crolos returned to her room and closed her diary.
