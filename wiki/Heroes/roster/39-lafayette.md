## Background
When they still lived in the forest, Bast would often visit Lafayette's small wooden cabin. To soothe the mind, Lafayette would light a stick of incense in the cabin and, when Bast arrived, tell her stories from the books he had read.

Bast always looked like she couldn't wake up properly. That day, in her dazed state, she was injured by a hidden trap in the forest. When she found Lafayette, she was extremely upset. While Lafayette searched for disinfectant, she played with a feather that had fallen on the ground. Bast watched in amazement as her wound gradually healed, and even the scar eventually disappeared.

Since mastering the method of healing wounds with feathers, Lafayette had always wanted to use this power to help more people. To this end, she sought out Elder Cliodna. "Junior, don't think of this world as too perfect. But if you truly want to realize your worth, go out and explore. Just don't come back crying."

Lafayette did not come back crying. When Cliodna heard that Lafayette had become the sixth knight of the Holy Knights, the corner of her mouth unconsciously twitched upward. Hmm, not a bad junior.
