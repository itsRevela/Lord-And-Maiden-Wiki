## Background
Merlinseath had prospered for twenty years thanks to the "Tree of Life," but the younger generation began to doubt Utia's story, dismissing it as mere legend. Then, on a stormy night, Utia quietly returned. In the town square, she left an ancient stone statue holding a gem called the "Heart of Time."

Utia told the townsfolk that this gem could foresee future crises. As she embedded the gem into the statue's chest, its eyes lit up, sending a beam of light shooting into the sky. With a final message - "When the Heart of Time flickers again, I shall return" - Utia vanished.

Several years later, Merlinseath was struck by drought, and the land became barren once more. Just as the townsfolk were losing hope, the Heart of Time flickered with a dazzling light. True to her word, Utia returned, leading the people to underground water sources and teaching them to harness the gem's power to regulate the climate.

From that day forward, Utia became the guardian of Merlinseath. Her story was no longer just a legend, but an eternal belief in the hearts of the townspeople. Whenever crisis loomed, the Heart of Time would flicker, and Utia's figure would appear as promised, protecting this land she so deeply loved.
