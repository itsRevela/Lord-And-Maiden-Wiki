## Background
In the Realm of Ice and Snow, survival is a difficult test. The temperature here is extremely low, the cold wind howls fiercely, and the land is covered with thick snow. It seems that every breath is a struggle against death. Barbarians and monsters run wild here. They are insatiably greedy and mercilessly devour all life.

Unfortunately, there is such a child. Her parents were successively exiled, all just to hide her in a safe place. Darby has never forgotten that cold night. The warmth of the campfire rescued her from despair.

The light of the fire shone on her face, bringing her a little warmth and hope. In those difficult days, Darby remembered the feeling of Duffy holding her in her arms. Duffy was her protector and her support.

That sense of security made her believe that no matter how cruel the world was, she had someone to rely on. With her love and courage, Duffy gave Darby the strength to persevere in the face of difficulties.
