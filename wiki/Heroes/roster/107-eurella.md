## Background
Eurera loves to play tricks, but her tricks are not subtle, and even full of loopholes. She enjoys fooling others, and sometimes she just conjures up furry ears or tails, or reveals her fangs in a cannibalistic expression, when she is pleasantly chatting with humans.

Among the warlike orcs, she likes hiding and hiding the most, and she doesn't like fighting. Eurella is very good at using her small body and unobtrusive hair to shuttle through the night, making it difficult for people to find. She also often used this method to flash in the dark, and then quickly disappeared, and then waited in the dark for others to look frightened.

But recently Eurera was a little annoyed, her pranks didn't work on the newcomer from another world, and she couldn't scare him even by turning into a werewolf's ferocious original form. Therefore, recently Eureira is even more elusive, trying to create some small movements.
