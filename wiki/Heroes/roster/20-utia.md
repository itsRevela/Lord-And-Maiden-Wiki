## Background
Twenty years ago, poverty in Merlinseath gradually spread to every corner of the town. Water was scarce, and the land could no longer sustain vegetation or crops. Many townsfolk fled to other places, and over time, it became the most impoverished area in the region.

On the day Utia arrived, she drew quite a bit of attention. The beautiful girl in a black dress clearly stood out from the surroundings, yet she showed no sign of discomfort. Instead, she held her head high and wandered around the town for quite some time. Later in the evening, someone spotted her in an open field, seemingly setting up an array or casting some kind of spell.

The next day, the entire town of Merlinseath was in an uproar. A towering tree had suddenly grown on the previously barren land, its lush green foliage forming a massive canopy, with clear water gushing out from its roots. Utia sat contentedly on a branch, distributing seeds to each townsfolk who came and carefully explaining how to plant them.

Twenty years passed, and Merlinseath flourished with the help of the "Tree of Life." Though Utia never appeared again during this time, stories about her continued to circulate in the alleyways. The most plausible theory suggested that the Tree of Life originated from some long-lost magic, and that the elven maiden belonged to an elusive clan of witches.
