# Hero Relics

**Relics** are hero-specific artifacts — one **"{Hero} Relic"** per hero — that **enhance that hero's [Talent Skill](Hero-Talents.md)** (e.g. a higher trigger chance, more damage, or extra healing). They are equipped in the hero's dedicated **Relic slot**. The Relic system (Relic Summon) unlocks at **Lord level 13**.

## How relics work
- **Levels:** a relic's power scales with its level (the **★4** heroes' relics go **Lv 1–4**, **★5** heroes' relics go **Lv 1–5**); each level is a stronger version of the same bonus.
- **Must be maxed to equip:** you can only equip a relic at its **maximum level**, and only on its matching hero — so the goal is always to raise a relic to Lv 4 (★4) / Lv 5 (★5) first.
- **Obtain (at Lv 1):** the **Relic Summon** gacha (spend a *Relic Summon Scroll*), Relic boxes (4★/5★, Random or Customize), or **combine 50 Ruby → one chosen 5★ relic**.
- **Upgrade (Lv 1 → max):** raise a relic in your inventory; the level-up material is **duplicate copies of the same relic**. *(The exact number of duplicates per level is enforced server-side and isn't in the game files.)*
- **Dismantle:** break a relic down into **Ruby**, which can be re-combined (50 Ruby) into a relic of your choice. *(Ruby yield per dismantle is server-side.)*
- A relic's in-game **Score** is `★ × level × 1800` (a maxed 5★ relic = 45,000; a maxed 4★ = 28,800).

*Bonuses below are "Not Affected By Attribute" where the game notes it (i.e. flat, not scaled by stats). Each row shows the bonus at **Lv 1** and at **max level**.*
