Events run on a repeating **12-day pattern**. Most have a simple objective that pays resources, speedups and **100 gems**, run for 2 days, and can be completed each day. Some add a **leaderboard** for extra end rewards; **exchange-shop** events (Vigorous Development) and **seasonal** events let you spend earned currency on a reward shop. A full calendar is in the in-game Events menu. *(Data-side configs on [Quests & Events](Quests-and-Events.md).)*
