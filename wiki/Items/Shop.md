# Shop (Recharge & Gift Packs)

The **Shop** is the in-game store for **paid** packs and gift bundles. Prices are in **Rand Coin** (the premium currency): real money buys Rand Coin in the Recharge panel, and Rand Coin is then spent on the packs below. It is a **separate panel from the [Market](Market.md)** (which spends earned currencies). The **Resets** column is how often a pack's purchase limit refills.
