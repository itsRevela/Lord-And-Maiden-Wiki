### Naval battle structures

- **Temporary Respawn Point** — A structure unlocked after occupying the Crystal. After death, the occupying team respawns from this structure and can also enter it to restore troops.
- **Landing Site** — Each color faction has its own Landing Site. When a team is wiped out, if the Crystal is not occupied, they respawn from the Landing Site. The Landing Site can also restore troops.
- **Dawnblade** — After occupying this structure, all teams of your faction gain the 'DMG Dealt Increased' effect.
- **Guardian Shield** — After occupying this structure, all teams of your faction gain the 'DMG Taken Reduced' effect.
- **Crystal** — The structure that determines victory in the battlefield. Occupying it unlocks the Temporary Respawn Point.
- **Victory Condition** — At the end of the event, the faction occupying this structure wins. If neither side occupies the Crystal, the faction with the higher total score wins. If scores are tied, both sides are considered defeated.
- **Outpost** — Traffic Node. If players from different factions are inside the Outpost, combat will trigger immediately.
- **Highlighted Outpost** — Among all Outposts, some are in a highlighted state. Entering such an Outpost grants continuous personal score.
