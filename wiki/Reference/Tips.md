# Game Tips

In-game loading tips and mechanics hints, grouped by topic.

### Formation
- The Commander Is The Core Of The Team; When The Commander Dies, The Battle Is Lost.
- Each team must be assigned a Commander, and the Commander's Strength must be greater than 0.
- When The Team Uses Heroes Of The Same Race, Additional Gain Effects Can Be Obtained.
- When The Team Uses Soldiers Of The Same Type, Additional Gain Effects Can Be Obtained.
- Target Selection (without Taunt):,Chance that Commander will be chosen as the target of ATK: 20% Chance that Striker 1 will be chosen as the target of ATK: 40% Chance that Striker 2 will be chosen as the target of ATK: 40%

### Skill
- Skill Activation Order: Passive Skill->Strategic Skill->Tactical Skill->Normal ATK->Pursuit Skill
- The Same Skill Effects Caused By Different Types Of Skills Can Stack, E.g., The Damage Increase Effect Of Strategic Skills And Tactical Skills Will Take Effect After Adding Them Together.
- The Same Skill Effects Caused By The Same Type Of Skills Cannot Stack, E.g., The Damage Increase Effect Of Two Strategic Skills Will Only Take The Maximum Value.
- Dodge, Assault, Instant, Superconducting, Eternal, Concentration, Taunts, Assist,Is A Unique Status Effect And Cannot Have The Same Effect Simultaneously.
- In The Skill Description, "2 Of Our Troops" Will Prioritize 2 Other Allied Troops Excluding Itself.
- The party cannot have 2 identical strategic skills, including those granted by skill stones.
- "Affected by X Attribute" in skill descriptions always refers to the caster (the hero equipped with the skill)
- The Healing Effect Is Affected By The Soldiers' HP Attribute
- Only soldiers with Slight Wound can be healed during combat; those with Severe Wound or Death cannot be healed
- The Chaos effect only applies to normal attacks and damage-dealing Tactical Skills. Subsequent Pursuit triggered by normal attacks is also affected.

### Soldier
- Soldiers Of Different Levels Have A Suppression Effect, And High-Level Soldiers Deal More Damage To Low-Level Soldiers
- Soldiers Of Different Types Have A Restraint Effect,Infantry->Archer->Cavalry->Infantry,When Soldiers Are Restrained, Damage Dealt Is Reduced By 25%.

### Hospital
- Slight Wounded Soldiers Will Immediately Recover To A Healthy State When They Return To The Camp.
- When Soldiers Are Severely Wounded, They Need To Go To The Hospital For Treatment.
- When Hospital's Capacity Is Insufficient, Severely Wounded Soldiers Will Die.
- When The Team's Commander Dies, A Large Number Of Severely Wounded Soldiers Will Be Generated.
- As combat continues, a certain percentage of Slight Wound soldiers will convert to Severe Wound or Death each round

### Residents
- Working affects Mood Points,Idle residents automatically recover Mood Points
- Working continuously over 8 hours affects Health Points
- Raiding Barbarian Camps will increase your Prestige Points.
- Higher Prestige Points makes Refugees more likely to join you.
- Expelling residents will decrease your Prestige Points.
- Residents Final Working Ability = Working Ability*Mood Points*Health Points
- Max Prestige Points = 5,000 Refugees Max Refresh Times = 3 + (Prestige Points / 1500) Refugees Refresh Time 30Min

### Great World
- All Targets Within The Nine-Square Grid Around The Player's Main City Are Occupiable.
- After Occupying The Core Mine, You Can Gain A Sphere Of Influence, And The Nine-Square Grids Adjacent To The Sphere Of Influence Are All Occupiable Targets.
- After Occupying The Mine, You Can Automatically Obtain Resources, But There Is A Time Limit. Upgrading The Transport Station Can Increase The Occupation Time And The Number Of Occupations.
- Occupation Rule: After Defeating All Enemies, It Belongs To The First Declarer Of War, So Friends Of The War Declarer Can Assist In Occupying The Target.
- Building A Camp Provides Long-Range Supply Capability.
- When offline for more than 14 days, your city in the Great World will be randomly relocated to another area

### Siege Battle
- Siege Battle requires creating/joining a Union, with the Leader or Deputy Leader declaring war.
- Siege Battle Is Based on Unions, And Need Any Ally's Sphere Of Influence Must Border The Target City To Be Attacked.
- In A Siege Battle, You Need To Defeat All The Defenders And Have Chariots Completely Destroy The City Walls Before You Can Finally Capture The City.
- When The Team Commander Has Chariot-type Soldiers, They Can Bombard Building
- All squads entering the Union Giant Trebuchet will automatically attack with it when the Siege Battle begins.
- The Giant Trebuchet's Destruction is determined by the combined Destruction Value of all squads.
- City occupation resets on the 1st of each month. Already occupied cities cannot be abandoned or reoccupied.

### Heroes Advance

- The maximum number of Advance times for a Hero is the same as the Hero's star rating.
