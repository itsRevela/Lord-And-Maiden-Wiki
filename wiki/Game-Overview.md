# Game Overview & Getting Started

**Lord and Maiden** is a strategy / city-management game: you grow a city of buildings, recruit and level a roster of named heroes, train four troop types, research technology, and fight through a PvE campaign, world & alliance bosses, trials and PvP — solo and in an alliance.
